About Us

Space City Tech is a Houston-based non-profit organization that is
fiscally sponsored by Hack Club. Our mission is to equip young
people in the Greater Houston area with the necessary skills to
succeed in the 21st-century workforce. We do this by offering free
computer science education and mentorship programs to students who
have a passion and aptitude for technology. At Space City Tech, we
believe that every child in Houston deserves the opportunity to learn
how to code, regardless of their background or financial situation. That's
why our services are open to everyone, and we work tirelessly to provide
access to high-quality computer science education to underserved communities.
By partnering with local schools and community organizations, we aim to create
a vibrant ecosystem of young technologists who are equipped with the
skills and knowledge they need to succeed in the digital age. Join us in
our mission to empower the next generation of leaders in Houston!
